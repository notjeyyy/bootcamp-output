Anita Max Wynn

Anita Max Wynn was introduced on a Kick livestream in December 2023. Depicted as a meme cartoon, the character is pictured with a red bob haircut and bangs, circular glasses, and a pink and yellow outfit. It is suspected that the original designer of the cartoon is someone who uses the named @createdbyseol on Instagram. Her name is inspired by Drake’s love for gambling, and loosely translates to “I need a max win,” which is a popular gambling term.

On Jan. 22, Drake released a collection of trucker hats with the Anita Max Wynn character on the front of it. The hats, which come in original aquamarine and black, also have the logo of the gambling company Stake on the side of it. Hats are currently on sale at DrakeRelated.com.
